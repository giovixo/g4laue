How to export geometry as a VRLM file:
/vis/open VRML2FILE: Opens a VRML2 output stream.
/vis/drawVolume: Draws the entire geometry to be exported.
/vis/viewer/flush: Finalizes the drawing.
/vis/sceneHandler/storeFile geometry.wrl: Exports the geometry to geometry.wrl.
